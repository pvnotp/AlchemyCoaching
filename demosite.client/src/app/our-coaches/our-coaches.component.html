Courtney Edwards
Courtney Edwards (she/her) is a counselor turned life, relationship, and career coach. She founded Alchemy Coaching in 2018 to help individuals, couples, and groups build a stronger foundation of wellbeing through the lens of positive psychology and mindfulness.

Courtney’s areas of specialty include: personal, career, and relationship satisfaction, burnout, executive functioning, habit change, and women's issues around care of self, communication, boundary setting, and empowerment.

Courtney offers individual services in person and remotely. Her relationship services for couples and polycules are available in person only.
Tarni Kaur

Tarni’s areas of specialty include: professionals, students, immigrants, neurodivergent individuals, and those navigating major life transitions.

Tarni is happy to offer remote services.

Individual Coaching

Frequent topics include: career exploration and planning, setting and holding boundaries, self-esteem, mindful living, burnout care and prevention, and creating more fulfilling relationships. This work will increase self-knowing and compassion, enhance effective emotional management, and allow for a greater capacity for happiness in your life.
